Woord Game Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Woord Game, we value your privacy. This document outlines our privacy practices and clarifies that no personal data is collected or stored when you use our app.

Data Collection

No personal data is collected. Woord Game does not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. We do not track usage patterns, gather analytics, or request permissions that allow access to personal details.

Children’s Privacy

We do not collect information from users of any age, including children under 13. As no data is gathered in any form, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of children’s data.

Third-Party Services

Since we do not collect any data, no third-party services (such as analytics or advertising networks) are employed within Woord Game.

Changes to This Policy

If we decide to introduce any feature in the future that may collect data, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly. Any changes will be highlighted in the app or on this page. By continuing to use Woord Game, you acknowledge the current version of this policy.
