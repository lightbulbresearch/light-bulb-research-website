About Light Bulb Research

Business-to-business AI and Machine Learning Solutions

Founded in 2012, Light Bulb Research is a leading provider of artificial intelligence and machine learning solutions based in Cape Town, South Africa. We specialize in delivering cutting-edge technology to help businesses harness the power of their data.

Data ROI Focus: Maximizing Your Data's Potential

At Light Bulb Research, we understand that your data is valuable. Our mission is to help you develop a data strategy that delivers a substantial return on your investment. Whether you're looking to:

  • Extract valuable insights from your data
  • Interact with your data in innovative ways
  • Implement predictive analytics

We have the expertise to maximize your data's potential. Our experience spans a wide range of machine learning solutions, including:

  • Unsupervised learning for clustering and pattern recognition
  • Supervised learning for accurate predictions
  • Deep learning techniques
  • Computer vision applications
  • Natural language processing
  • Generative AI solutions

Our Esteemed Clients

Naspers

A South African multinational media and internet group operating in over 130 countries. Naspers is one of the largest companies on the JSE by market capitalization and a constituent of the FTSE/JSE Africa Top 40 index.

Shoprite

The largest grocery retailer in South Africa, Shoprite operates a vast network of over 2,800 stores across 15 African countries. Our solutions help Shoprite optimize their operations and enhance customer experiences.

University of Cape Town (UCT)

Established in 1829, UCT is the oldest university in South Africa and consistently ranked as one of the top universities in Africa. We collaborate with UCT to advance research in AI and machine learning.

National Research Foundation (NRF)

A statutory body established by the South African government to support and promote research and innovation. We work with the NRF to develop AI solutions that address South Africa's development needs and contribute to economic and social progress.
