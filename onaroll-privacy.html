On a Roll

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how On a Roll ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use the app on your iOS device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users of the app.

The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any third-party services that collect data about you.

Device Motion Data

The app uses your device's motion sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope) solely to control the ball movement during gameplay. This data is processed on your device in real time and is never stored, transmitted, or shared.

Game Center

The app integrates with Apple's Game Center to provide leaderboards. When you use Game Center features, your Game Center nickname and score are submitted to Apple's servers and displayed in the global leaderboard.

Game Center data is managed by Apple according to the Apple Privacy Policy. We do not have access to any personal information from your Apple ID or Game Center account.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from children. It is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
