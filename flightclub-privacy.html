Privacy Policy

Flight Club for iOS — Effective 30 March 2026

The short version

Flight Club stores all data locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers or any third party. We have no servers. We collect no analytics, run no ads, and have no tracking of any kind.

What data the app uses

Flight Club uses your device's sensors to record flight data. The following information is captured during a flight and stored on your device only:

Data Source Purpose
GPS coordinates, altitude, speed, heading Device GPS Flight path recording and map display
Pitch, roll, acceleration Device motion sensors Aircraft attitude and G-force logging
Pilot name, co-pilot name Manual entry Flight record keeping
Aircraft registration, airports Manual entry Flight record keeping
Weather conditions Manual entry Pre-flight documentation

Where data is stored

All flight data is stored in a local database on your device. Data never leaves your device through the app. There is no cloud sync, no remote backup, and no server-side component.

If you delete the app, all flight data is permanently removed.

Location services

Flight Club requests access to your device's location to record your flight path using GPS. Location data is:

  • Only collected while you are actively recording a flight
  • Stored locally on your device
  • Never transmitted to any external service

You can deny location access at any time through your device's Settings. The app will not be able to record flight paths without it.

Motion & sensor data

Flight Club uses your device's accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnetometer to record aircraft attitude (pitch and roll) and acceleration during flight. This sensor data is stored locally and never shared.

Third-party services

Flight Club uses no third-party services. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, crash reporters, or external APIs.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any of your data to third parties. Since all data stays on your device and we have no access to it, sharing is not possible.

Children's privacy

Flight Club does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require an account or collect any personal information beyond what the user manually enters for their own flight records.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Material changes that affect how your data is handled will be communicated through an app update.
